### [Check it out here!](https://baoware.github.io/assets/projects/movies/index.html)

A D3.js powered svg which displays all the movies I have watched. Only has viewing functionalities for each movie and sorting by rating as of now, but I am working on sorting by genre.



Now with a working svg, I don't think it is the most meaningful visualization. Partly because of how little data, Letterboxd provides with their user data downloads but also my lack of creativity. However, I am still happy that it was a great learning experience with D3.js and svg's.
